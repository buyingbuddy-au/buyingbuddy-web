# Buying Buddy — Competitor Battle Card

## The Landscape

### Carify (carify.com.au)
- **Price:** $22.99 (Essentials), $31.99 (Comprehensive), $36.99 (Ultimate)
- **What they check:** PPSR, stolen, write-off, rego, odometer, valuation, safety recalls
- **Unique features:** Visual write-off damage report showing WHERE the car was damaged, automated finance updates (6 checks/day for 14 days)
- **Weakness:** No human review. No negotiation advice. No contract templates. Purely automated database lookups.
- **Market position:** Premium automated reports. Slick UI.

### CarHistory (carhistory.com.au)
- **Price:** $11.99 (Basic), $19.99 (Standard), $29.99 (Comprehensive)
- **What they check:** PPSR, stolen, write-off, rego history, valuation
- **Unique features:** "Nearly 1 million reports" social proof. Compare two cars side by side.
- **Weakness:** Same as Carify — automated only. No human insight. Generic.
- **Market position:** Mid-market. Established brand.

### PPSR.gov.au (official)
- **Price:** $2 per search
- **What they check:** PPSR only (finance/security interests)
- **Weakness:** Raw data. No interpretation. Confusing for consumers. No rego, stolen, or write-off data.
- **Market position:** Government source. Cheapest but hardest to use.

### RedBook (redbook.com.au)
- **Price:** Free basic valuation
- **What they check:** Market value estimates only
- **Weakness:** No vehicle history. No PPSR. Just pricing.
- **Market position:** Valuation reference. Used by dealers and banks.

### CarsGuide Valuations
- **Price:** Free
- **What they check:** Market value estimates
- **Weakness:** Same as RedBook — no history checks
- **Market position:** Consumer reference

## Our Competitive Advantages

### 1. Free entry point
Nobody else offers a free listing check. Carify's cheapest is $22.99. We give away the first taste for free and upsell from there.

### 2. Human expertise
Every competitor is fully automated. None of them can look at a listing and say "this looks like a wholesale yard posing as a private seller" or "the photos show panel gaps on the driver's side — possible unreported accident." That's what 15 years of dealer experience gives you.

### 3. Cheapest PPSR in Australia
$4.95 vs $11.99+ from competitors. We undercut everyone because our cost is only $2. This is the gateway drug to higher-value products.

### 4. Actionable advice, not just data
Competitors give you data and leave you to figure it out. We tell you what to DO with the data — what to offer, what to say, what to check when you get there.

### 5. QLD-specific private sale support
Nobody provides a QLD private sale contract template. Nobody walks you through the paperwork. This is a genuine gap in the market.

### 6. Post-purchase support
The $34.95 tier includes 48-hour text support. No competitor offers any human contact during the purchase process.

## Pricing Strategy

- **Free tier** captures emails and builds trust — no competitor does this
- **$4.95 PPSR** is a loss-leader that undercuts everyone and converts browsers to buyers
- **$14.95 Dealer Review** is the core revenue product — unique in the market
- **$34.95 Full Pack** captures the high-anxiety buyer who wants everything handled

The total stack ($4.95 + $14.95 + $34.95) is competitive with Carify's top tier ($36.99) but includes human review, negotiation scripts, and a contract template that Carify doesn't offer at any price.
