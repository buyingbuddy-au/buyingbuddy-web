Payment Methods: Safe Options for Private Deals

How to pay without losing your money or your mind.

Forget cash in a bag — it’s dangerous and untraceable. Bank transfer with the seller present at your bank is safest; both parties can confirm receipt before handing over keys. Bank cheque is another option, but confirm it can’t bounce. Avoid PayPal “friends and family” payments; they offer no buyer protection. Never pay via gift cards or cryptocurrency. Whatever you choose, ensure the transfer happens at the same time as signing the contract.
