Understanding NEVDIS and Why It Matters

The National Exchange of Vehicle and Driver Information System reveals more than you think.

NEVDIS holds registration, stolen status and write‑off details from across Australia. When you run a PPSR, the result taps into NEVDIS to show if a vehicle has been reported stolen, has odometer inconsistencies or registration discrepancies. Always review the NEVDIS section carefully. If the make or model doesn’t match the ad, suspect a rebirth. If the odometer reading is lower than a previous record, it’s been wound back.
