Buying with Finance: What to Watch For

If you need a loan to buy a used car, avoid these traps.

Shop around before taking dealer finance; third‑party lenders often have better rates. Read the fine print: some loans have early payout penalties or balloon payments. Make sure the loan term isn’t longer than the car’s expected life. Consider pre‑approval so you can negotiate like a cash buyer. And always confirm that any encumbrance from the previous owner is cleared before your lender pays out.
