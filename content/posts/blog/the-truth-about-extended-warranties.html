The Truth About Extended Warranties on Used Cars

Do they offer peace of mind or are they just sales fluff?

Extended warranties are often sold as insurance against expensive repairs, but many exclude common issues and require servicing at specified centres. Read the Product Disclosure Statement carefully; you may find more exclusions than cover. Sometimes an emergency fund or comprehensive insurance is better value. If you do buy one, negotiate the price down — these products have high profit margins.
