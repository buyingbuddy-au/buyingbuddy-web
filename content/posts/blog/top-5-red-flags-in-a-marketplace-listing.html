Top 5 Red Flags in a Marketplace Listing

Don’t fall for the most common traps. These warning signs mean it’s time to scroll on.

  1. No clear VIN or rego visible. Hidden numbers hide hidden problems.
  2. Stock photos or blurry images. Always demand real photos in daylight.
  3. Price far below market value. Too cheap usually means too risky.
  4. Sellers who won’t meet or call. Face‑to‑face is non‑negotiable.
  5. Urgency or sob stories (“I’m moving overseas tomorrow”). Pressure tactics are classic scam signals.
