Private vs Dealer: Which Offers More Protection?

The pros and cons of buying from a private seller versus a licensed dealer.

Dealers must provide a statutory warranty on many used cars and are regulated by consumer laws. You may pay more but you get recourse if something fails. Private sellers usually offer lower prices but no warranty, and you buy “as is.” With the right tools and knowledge, private sales can be safe and save you thousands, but you bear all the risk. Decide what matters more: price or peace of mind.
