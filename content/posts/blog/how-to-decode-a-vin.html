How to Decode a VIN Like a Pro

Break down the 17‑digit Vehicle Identification Number and what each section means.

A VIN isn’t random. Digits 1–3 tell you the country and manufacturer, 4–9 describe the model and body type, and 10–17 are unique to the vehicle. Always cross‑check the VIN on the dash, door jamb and rego papers. A mismatch could signal theft. Use online VIN decoders to confirm make and model; if the seller’s description doesn’t match, something’s wrong.
