# Parked Concept — Buyers Advocate (archived 16 April 2026)

## Summary

`buyers-advocate.vercel.app` was a standalone single-page site deployed on 01 April 2026 that positioned Jordan as a full-service used car **Buyer's Advocate** in Brisbane / SEQ, charging a **flat $497 fee** to inspect, valuate, negotiate, and facilitate private car purchases on behalf of clients.

It was a concierge/service offering — the opposite of Buying Buddy's locked plan (low-cost self-serve toolkit). It had **no GitHub repo**, no custom domain, no organic traffic, and no sales. Two deployments in total, both on the same day, no activity since.

**Env vars at delete time:** `TELEGRAM_BOT_TOKEN`, `TELEGRAM_CHAT_ID` — wired to send Telegram notifications on form submission. Destroyed with the project.

## Neglect signal — "Jordan Ladsbury" typo

The surname is spelled **"Ladsbury"** (missing the `n`) throughout the entire site — headline, intro copy, FAQ, footer, meta. Correct spelling is **Lansbury**. The site was AI-generated, never proof-read, and never QA'd by Jordan. A symptom of an experiment that was shipped to Vercel and forgotten, not a concept that earned follow-through.

## Parking note

**This concept is PARKED.**

The buyer's advocate model **conflicts with Buying Buddy's locked plan** (locked 2026-04-16). Buying Buddy is a low-cost, self-serve toolkit — not a concierge or service business. The anti-drift rules explicitly forbid "buyer's agent / concierge positioning on any public-facing page" and "services that require Jordan's live time without explicit approval" and "premium pricing tiers."

However, the underlying **concept** may return as a **feature inside Buying Buddy** later — specifically as:

- The **hidden $99 Quick Review** post-purchase upsell (already defined in the locked plan)
- A future **sourcing add-on** or **inspection booking** service (mentioned in Phase 3 of the locked plan)

It will NOT return as a standalone product. Do NOT resurrect the buyers-advocate.vercel.app concept, positioning, or pricing structure without explicit approval from Jordan.
