We don't need millions of visitors. We need 20 leads/month from the right people.

## Gap Opportunities (Underserved Searches)

### Gap 1: "wholesale car buyer Brisbane"
- **Volume:** Low (30–80/month) but extremely high intent
- **Why it matters:** A buyer searching this term knows what wholesale pricing means. They're sophisticated and looking for an insider edge. This is BuyingBuddy's perfect customer.
- **Action:** Write a dedicated landing page and blog post explaining the wholesale-retail gap and how Jordan bridges it. Use real examples (without specific deal details).
- **Suggested URL:** `/wholesale-car-prices-brisbane`

### Gap 2: "avoid getting ripped off buying car Brisbane"
- **Volume:** Low (20–60/month) but very high emotional intent
- **Why it matters:** This is fear-based search — the exact pain point BuyingBuddy solves. Nobody is targeting it with a proper page.
- **Action:** Long-form guide (1,500–2,000 words) covering the 7 biggest rip-off tactics used in Brisbane car sales, with BuyingBuddy as the solution at the bottom.
- **Suggested URL:** `/avoid-car-buying-scams-brisbane`

### Gap 3: "car buying checklist Brisbane" / "questions to ask when buying a used car"
- **Volume:** Medium (200–500/month)  
- **Why it matters:** People in research mode who are about to make a decision. A downloadable checklist is a lead magnet that converts well.
- **Action:** Create a free downloadable "Brisbane Used Car Buyer's Checklist" — 20 questions to ask before handing over cash. Collect email addresses. Use as nurture sequence entry point.

### Gap 4: "car buyer's agent Queensland"
- **Volume:** Low but growing as buyer's agent concept becomes mainstream
- **Why it matters:** State-level term with low competition. BuyingBuddy can own QLD before anyone else does.
- **Action:** Create a /queensland page covering BuyingBuddy's reach (Brisbane-focused but can assist across SEQ).
