## SECTION 6 — THE CONTRACT ANGLE

**Label:** Buying Privately?

**Headline:** Most people have no idea what paperwork they need.

**Body:**
In Queensland, private car sales have almost no consumer protection. There's no cooling-off period. No warranty. No dealer to go back to if something's wrong.

That's why every Buying Buddy Full Confidence Pack includes a QLD-specific private sale contract template — ready to print, fill in, and sign on the day. It covers:

- Buyer and seller details
- Vehicle identification (VIN, rego, odometer)
- Purchase price and payment method
- Disclosure of known defects
- Safety certificate confirmation
- Settlement terms

Don't show up with a handshake and a bank transfer. Show up with a contract.
